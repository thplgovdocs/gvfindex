Index to the Genealogy Vertical File Collection located at the Florida History and Genealogy Library.

This is an index to the Genealogy Vertical File Collection located at the Florida History and Genealogy Library. The Genealogy Vertical File Collection is composed of the family charts of previous members of the Florida Genealogical Society, Tampa Chapter. The Florida History and Genealogy Library is located on the 4th floor of the John F. Germany Public Library.
